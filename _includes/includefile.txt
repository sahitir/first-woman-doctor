Dr. Kadambini Ganguly and Dr. Anandi Gopal Joshi inspired many Indian women to pursue education, even study and practice medicine.

Seen here are Anandi Gopal with two other ladies who trained as physicians with her and were the first women from their respective countries to pursue medicine.
